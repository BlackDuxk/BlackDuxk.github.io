The Process

The process of getting a book banned is not an easy one. To get a specific book banned, either in a school or library, a school or library must first recieve a substantial amount of challenges for the given book. After that it's up to the given institution's disgretion to either ban access to the book or not.

Step 1

Someone challenges a book.

Step 2

Libraries, across the U.S. report all challenged books. to

Step 3

The American Lbrary Association (ALA) recieves these reports/challenges, and keeps track of all challenges.

Step 4

The institution, school or library, bans access to the book based on the challenges it recieves regarding it based own its own disgretion.
